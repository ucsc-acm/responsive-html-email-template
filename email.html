Reminder for Tomorrow's Meeting

Dear ACM,

We have a meeting tomorrow (11/8) at 5:30-6:30PM at E2-180. It is not in E2-599 this time.

For the meeting tomorrow, we will have a member run meeting by Stephen Pinkerton. Stephen will be presenting his talk on "Scrappy Job Hunting, Resume Writing, and Other Unsolicited Advice". I know that this is going to be very helpful for everyone. The following is a description of Stephen's talk:

"Come learn how to be scrappy in finding your dream internship/job in software engineering or product management! We'll cover everything from freshman to full-time recruiting, resume writing, finding interesting companies, startups, working abroad, getting your application in front of a real person, diversity organizations, compensation, negotiating offers, and personal finance for high-earning new grads.

The speaker, Stephen Pinkerton, is a final year computer science student at UCSC who has worked in software engineering, embedded systems, and product management at Facebook, Google, Square, Cloudflare, and Monzo."

Additionally, Stephen will look at your resumes and review them on the spot only if you bring a printed copy.
